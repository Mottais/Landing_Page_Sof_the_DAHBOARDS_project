DASHBOARDS

Tool allowing visualization and real-time update of JARNOT company projects progress anywhere, by all stakeholders.

Features

Employee Account Management

Grant application access to your employees and control their permissions based on their roles.

To add, remove, or modify accounts, simply add, remove, or modify rows in an Excel file.

To add, remove, or modify profiles, simply add, remove, or modify columns in the same spreadsheet.

Then assign profile(s) to each account.

Definition of Company Production steps

To add, remove, or modify company production steps, simply add, remove, or modify columns in an Excel file.

Assign the necessary profile to each step using a dropdown list updated automatically with the list of profiles managed in account management.

Definition of Project Packages

Packages are defined in the SEMA software.

This ensures consistency between design and production.

No adaptation is necessary. The company's design office staff designs projects in the software as they normally do.

Adding Project to Application

From SEMA, export the project to Excel. Then from the created file:

- Sort project packages in chronological order of production.

- Define operations to be performed or not.

- You can add a message to each operation for the operators who will perform it.

- Then click on the [ADD PROJECT] button.

Company Intranet Login

Each employee with a created account can view the progress of all company projects.

This can be done from the office, workshops, and construction sites via a tablet and even their phone.

Display of Current Projects

All unfinished projects and projects completed within the last 7 days.

This prevents forgetting projects that are not quite finished...

Real-time Visualization and Modification of Project Progress

Each employee can report their work progress.

Supervisors, workshop managers, project managers, team leaders, ... all have an overview of company projects and their progress. They can react more quickly if the progress is not as expected, implement corrective actions, and adjust the schedule if necessary.

About

Since 2018, I have held the position of methodist for the BAM Group. JARNOT is a carpentry and roofing company within the group.

To date, dashboards already exist in paper format. These dashboards were initiated 4 years ago following regular observations that some 'packages' were not ready on time or incomplete. These are situations that must be avoided at all costs as they are very costly, especially during the 'installation' steps on site (construction site).

These dashboards have significantly reduced such issues and improved work organization. The worker must check a step when completed.

However, this solution has several disadvantages:

  • Paper support does not lend itself well to sharing; several workers may need it at the same time. As a result, the document is not always well updated.
  • Information is anonymous, which does not foster accountability among personnel.
  • It is a (paper) medium that can be lost or damaged, especially in this sector exposed to weather conditions.
  • There is a significant delay in reporting project progress to the supervisor. Often, they have to wait until the end of the day to be informed. This complicates planning and adjustments.

By developing a web application that digitizes the paper document, all these drawbacks are resolved.
